NGAU LEARNING GUIDE

How to Play Ngau (牛牛玩法)

Ngau（牛牛）每局會拿到 5 張牌。核心目標是把牌分成「下排 3 張底牌」和「上排 2 張點牌」，先確保底牌總和可被 10 整除，再追求最高牌型。 This page is a quick bilingual walkthrough so new players can start correctly in minutes.

How to Play Rules 3/6 Swap Example Rounds FAQ

1) Basic Round Flow

  1. Each player gets 5 cards.
  2. Choose 3 cards as the base (底牌) and 2 cards as point cards (點牌).
  3. The base must be divisible by 10. If not, the split is invalid.
  4. Once the base is valid, evaluate the 2 point cards to determine hand strength.

Quick Input Format

在本網站計算器，你可以輸入類似 AS 3 6 8 K。A 可帶花色，例如 AS（黑桃 A），其餘牌可直接輸入牌面。 10 可輸入成 10。

New Player Priorities

  1. First priority: make a valid base (sum divisible by 10).
  2. Second priority: maximize the point-hand result.
  3. Third priority: consider special outcomes such as 孖寶 and 牛冬菇.

Tip: If you struggle to see valid base combinations quickly, use the calculator to train your pattern recognition. After 20 to 30 rounds, speed improves a lot.

2) What Makes a Strong Result

本計算器會依照牌型倍數、點數與 kicker 做比較。一般來說，特殊牌型（例如 牛冬菇、五張公）會優先於普通點數。 If two outcomes look close, the app always picks the stronger legal arrangement automatically.

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Forgetting that base must be valid before point cards matter.
  • Ignoring the 3/6 swap possibility when searching for better splits.
  • Treating all Ace cards as special; only black spade Ace (AS) triggers 牛冬菇 directly.
