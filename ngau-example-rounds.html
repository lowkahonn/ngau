Ngau Example Rounds (實戰例子)

這些例子用真實流程示範：先驗證底牌，再比較點牌，最後選出最佳結果。你可以直接把牌組輸入計算器，逐步對照理解。

How to Play Rules 3/6 Swap Example Rounds FAQ

Round 1: Straightforward Valid Split

Hand: AS 9 K 4 7

  1. Try base: 9 + 4 + 7 = 20, valid base.
  2. Point cards: AS + K = 牛冬菇 condition satisfied.
  3. Result becomes a high-priority special hand.

Takeaway

有 AS 配 J/Q/K 時，通常值得先測試能否湊出合法底牌。只要底牌合法，牛冬菇往往會成為最有力結果。

Round 2: 3/6 Swap Unlocks Better Result

Hand: 3 6 8 J K

  1. Without swap, some base choices are weak or invalid.
  2. Swap one of 3/6 states and retry base combinations.
  3. A new valid base appears, and point cards can move to stronger output.

If a hand looks close but not quite legal, 3/6 swap is often the first lever to test.

Round 3: Pair Versus 10 Points Decision

Hand: Q Q 2 8 K

  1. Base 2 + 8 + K(10) = 20 is valid.
  2. Point pair Q + Q forms 孖寶 with multiplier advantage.
  3. Even if another split can produce 10 points, multiplier comparison may still favor pair.

Takeaway

不要只看「點數看起來高」。實際比較時，倍數權重很關鍵，孖寶常常能勝過看似不錯的普通點數。
